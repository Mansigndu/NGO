OUR STORY!!!

WE FOR A CAUSE FOUNDATION (WFC) WE FOR A CAUSE FOUNDATION (WFC) is a NGO started in 2019 by group of friends and young volunteers who are now the change makers. Coco was the inspiration behind starting this NGO, he was abandoned and was found by us in August 2019. We tried contacting several Animal help group but no one showed up we decided to do something ourselves and we did succeed in helping Coco and saving his life. He will be always our First Rescue of WFC. Later on, we found many animals who needed help, we faced several difficulties while helping these animals and so we decided to register this NGO so that we could work on a large scale.

From a family of 3 to 50 now, We work for the well-being of animals suffering on the streets. As the name suggests, "WE" no one can bring change alone. We are constantly working towards bringing a positive change in the Environment, Society, and Lives of animals. We are here for a change. Our logo speaks to the purpose of this NGO that we work in the Environment, Animal Welfare, and also work on the social issues that are prevailing in society and we want to change the mindset of the society.

WHAT WE DO?

Animal Welfare

We rescue, feed and treat stray animals who are born on streets with no shelter, food or care. We work together to bring peace and happiness to these voiceless souls.

Women & Child Support

We focus on women empowerment and child education. We conduct awareness campaigns and arrange free self-defense sessions in high-risk areas.

Transgender Empowerment

We believe everyone has equal rights. We actively work towards transgender awareness and empowerment in society.

Community Support

During lockdown, we fed 800+ animals and supported 150+ families. We continue expanding our efforts to serve humanity.
