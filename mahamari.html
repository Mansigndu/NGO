MAHWAARI (Sanskrit term for Menstruation)

Menstrual Hygiene and Period Poverty

Menstrual hygiene is a very important for every female as this can decrease their risk of menstrual infections. Unhygienic menstruation can have adverse effects on overall sexual and reproductive health of women's. And so a healthy menstruation is every women's basic right.

We at We For A Cause Foundation will arrange awareness session in remote areas on menstrual health importance, and will ensure that every women has a right to healthy menstruation and will tackle period poverty in Rural areas where there is lack of sanitation and hygiene.

Join us in our journey and help us arrange a menstrual hygiene session in your village for healthy menstruation.
