Women empowerment & Transgender empowerment.

Empowering Women is Developing Nation !

We For A Cause works for women's rights and a safe, empowered environment for the transgender community because these groups are denied basic rights. We arrange campaigns to raise awareness and encourage the education of girls.

Transgender Empowerment - Transgender community deserves the dignity and respect that most people take for granted. What you are born with doesn't matter; what counts is who you grow up to be. Particularly in India, the transgender community is mistreated. Every person deserves respect, so let's change our perspective towards them.

Self -Defense Training - We work for women's safety because we believe every woman should be able to protect herself in an emergency, we advocate for women's safety and provide free self-defense training sessions.

Women Empowerment - We work hard to educate women about their rights and the potential benefits of joining one of our self-help groups. We help women build the core skills necessary for employment and independence. This will lessen the likelihood of domestic abuse and marital rapes.

What We Do?

Women's have right to be safe, educated and independent. Empowered woman influences her environment.

WOMEN EMPOWERMENT

Being born as a transgender is not a choice. The first step toward transformation is to modify our perspective, therefore show them respect.

TRANSGENDER AWARENESS

We can't stop rapist from roaming around but we can prevent ourselves from getting raped. Book your training sessions with us and boost your confidence to fight any situations.

SELF DEFENSE

Women are driving social change. Join us in our mission to stop female infanticide, rape, domestic abuse, child marriage, and to support girl child education to make every women independent.
