PROJECT SAATH

Project Saath is our new initiative taken up by WFC Foundation for improving the lives of children and women by educating and empowering them. We focus on making the future generation independent and helping our country grow on the path of development.

MISSION PADHAI - Our mission is educating poor children free of cost. You can donate old books, textbooks and encyclopedias. Currently 15 children are taking education under Mission Padhai and we aim to expand more.

MISSION VASTRA - Donate your old clothes and bedsheets to people in need. Torn clothes are reused to make blankets and dog beds. We believe in reuse and recycle.No clothes will go in waste. You can donate to the needy instead of storing for years.

MISSION BHOOKH - Feeding is a best thing a person can do. We see lot of food getting waste in marriages and events, so Mission Bhookh is a initiative started by us. We collect food from such functions and feed it to poor persons in need instead of throwing it somewhere in gutters the food might fill the stomach of needy.
